What we do ?

Energy Analytics

We acquire data from your enterprise and analyze it using our sophisticated software to deliver useful insights that are practical and easy to implement.

Energy Auditing

An energy audit involves the process of identifying areas of energy wastage in your organization. A certified energy auditor provides a comprehensive method for achieving energy and cost saving opportunities.

Power Quality Analysis

Power Quality standards such as IEEE 519, IEC 61000 -4-30, EN 50160, IEC 61000 -2-4 are essential in monitoring power quality. Identifying faults, its location, its time, its duration, its cause and frequency of such faults could provide insights on how to mitigate repetition and arrest such faults from happening again. This ensures asset equipment protection for longer productivity with zero interruptions. Resulting in a predictive and preventive maintenance operations.
